🛕 Ancient History

• Maurya Dynasty (322–185 BCE)

The Maurya Empire was one of the world’s largest empires under Emperor Ashoka who spread Buddhism across Asia.

• Gupta Dynasty (319–543 CE)

This era is known as the Golden Age of India – great achievements in mathematics, literature, and astronomy.

• British Raj & Independence Movement (1858–1947)

India struggled under British rule but achieved freedom on 15 August 1947 after a long non-violent resistance.

💡 Indian Inventions

  • Zero (0) – Invented by Indian mathematician Aryabhata.
  • Chess – Originated as *Chaturanga* in ancient India.
  • Ayurveda – A 5,000-year-old medical science.
  • Cotton Weaving – India was the first to produce and weave cotton.

🌟 Famous Indian Figures

• Chandragupta Maurya

Founder of the Maurya Empire, united most of India under one rule.

• Ashoka the Great

Promoted peace and Buddhist values across Asia.

• Mahatma Gandhi

Leader of India’s independence movement, known for his philosophy of non-violence.

🏺 Indian Artifacts

• Ashoka Pillar

The Lion Capital, now India’s National Emblem, symbolizes unity and courage.

• Indus Valley Seals

Seals used for trade, showing early signs of writing and civilization.
